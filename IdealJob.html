My Ideal Job

Job Add on Seek

My ideal job would be to work as a help desk engineer . It would allow me to use my skills in IT to trouble shoot and fix problems for clients both face to face and over the phone . This is something I enjoy doing and would suit my personality and aptitude for practical skills developed during my training as both an aircraft mechanical engineer and diesel fitter . I am more interested in the application of practical skills than theory although I recognise the need for relevant background knowledge.

What is Technical Support ?
Also known as: Helpdesk support, operations analyst, problem manager.
This job in brief: These are the professional troubleshooters of the IT world. Many technical support specialists work for hardware manufacturers and suppliers solving the problems of business customers or consumers, but many work for end-user companies supporting, monitoring and maintaining workplace technology and responding to users' requests for help. Some lines of support require professionals with specific experience and knowledge, but tech support can also be a good way into the industry for graduates.
Key skills required: Wide ranging tech knowledge, problem solving, communication/listening, patience and diplomacy.

The skills, qualifications and experience required for this job would be to have good oral and written skills , good inter-personal skills to be able to deal with clients and co-workers , the ability to trouble shoot and come up with solutions to fit the client's needs , hardwear and softwear trouble shooting skills encluding VoIP, ability to manage time and multitask and experience working with a variety of softwear and hardwear . The Softwear use I would need experience in for this position includes Microsoft Office , Microsoft Office 365 Admin , MYOB , Reckon , QuickBooks , Windows Server Active Directory and Group Policy , IT Glue , Veeam , StorageCraft , Yealink and Grandstream .

While I currently have good oral and written skills , good inter-personal skills and the ability to manage time and multitask , I have limited knoweledge of the technical skills and relevant background information required for this position .

To gain the skills, qualifications and experience that I would require to be able to trouble shoot and come up with solutions to fit the clients' needs I would need experience in that area from working in an entry level position utilising similar softwear and hardwear. I would require experience in trouble shooting hardwear and softwear which I intend to gain from the entry level position . This degree will give me exposure to some of the softwear required and the background knowelege and framework which I currently do not have and will allow me to grain an entry level position , while giving me general knowledge in all other aspects of the IT industry as a whole . The units Introduction to Computer Systems , Security in Computing and IT and Softwear Engineering Fundamentals for IT specifically will help me in achieving the required level of skills needed for this position.
